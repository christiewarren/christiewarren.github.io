Thoughts on Photography
My love for photography began about seven years ago, and since then it's only gotten stronger. I've gone from capturing mostly landscapes to photographing people, events, animals, and more. Nature never ceases to amaze me, and that's what I love to capture most, but I always remind myself to put my camera down for a moment to take it all in through my owneyes. A camera can capture incredible things, but nothing comes close to actually living them.

Please enjoy this collection of the wonderful people, places, and things I've been lucky to photograph.
