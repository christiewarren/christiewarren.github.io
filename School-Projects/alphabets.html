Found Alphabets

Objective: Photograph two alphabets: one using "intentional" letters (ie. packaging, signs, etc.) and one using unintentional letters (ie. anything and everything that happens to be shaped like a letter. Then, create a word from either of the alphabets that encompasses the theme of the letters.

Created with: Canon Rebel T3i, Lightroom, Photoshop, & InDesign.
