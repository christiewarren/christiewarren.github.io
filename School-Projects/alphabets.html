Found Alphabets

The objective of this project was to find and photograph an entire alphabet made of intentional letters and another of unintentional letters. I spent a TON of time on this project, photographing most of the letters in the unintentional piece at Staples, and most of the intentional ones at Target. I'm actually really happy with how these turned out, and how they look together. It's surprising how difficult it was to make these pieces look cohesive together with the many fonts and colors in the world, all meant to stand out and not blend in with one another. This project certainly taught me a lot about persistence and how it can make a huge difference in the quality of a piece.
