Beauty Abroad

For this project, our class was actually lucky enough to have the real clients of Global Penn State, who run all of the marketing for Study Abroad programs. We were presented with a few objectives to pick from, and the one I chose was to advertise less common countries to study in. The medium we used to create the posters was completely open ended, and I chose to photograph flowers and then image trace them in Illustrator to create a painted affect.
