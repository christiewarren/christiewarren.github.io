The Sock

This was the final project of my first year in graphic design, due the same week as our portfolio review to get accepted into the program. Needless to say it was quite a stressful week, but I'm really proud of the outcome of the project. I feel like this demonstrates my style fairly well in that it's really simple and fun to look through. I also loved adding the physical touch of the drawer, which I made by making an origami box and covering it with thin pieces of wood.
