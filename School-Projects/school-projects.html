Graphic Design at Penn State
Being in such a tiny program (20 people per graduating class) at such a huge school (46,000 students at University Park) is a really unique experience. One of the most valuable (and my personal favorite) parts of the program is the many, many critiques we do. Being able to receive and actually use criticism is such an important skill to learn, and I find myself getting better and better at doing so each semester. I also love analyzing the other projects in critiques and problem solving with my peers to figure out how we help to make them better. Not to mention, in such a small program, our group of 20 has become tight knit, and I'm really happy to be a part of it all.
