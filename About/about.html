About Me

Hey again, thanks for stopping by!

Who Am I?
I'm a product designer (UI, UX, and everything in between) from Schwenksville, PA (a suburb near Philly). I study Graphic Design at Penn State, University Park, and I'm in my third year here. I'm a huge fan of John Mulaney (take a few minutes to watch that, you deserve a good laugh), chick pea-based snacks, and sunflowers.

What do I do?
Classes, freelance, and personal projects keep me pretty busy. But when I do have free time, I love rock climbing (bouldering), photography, petting my dogs and cats, and learning as many new things as I can. Right now, I'm working to learn Javascript and JQuery so that I can make my sites more interesting and pleasant to interact with. Oh, and sleep. I try to do that sometimes.

What do I stand for?
Technology is an essential part of nearly everyone's lives these days. My thought is this: designing and developing websites and software that care about their users and strive to make little interactions a bit more enjoyable holds immense power in making the entire human experience a whole lot better.

I was recently in a very nicely-kept hotel bathroom, and it occurred to me how appreciated I felt that they took the time to make even the bathroom pleasant. I want users to feel appreciated, and so I try to pay extra attention to the less-glamorous aspects of their experience -- the UX of Bathrooms, if you will.
